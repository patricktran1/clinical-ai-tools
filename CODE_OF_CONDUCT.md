# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Clinical Evidence Guardrails welcomes respectful, evidence-oriented collaboration across clinical, engineering, research, accessibility, and safety backgrounds.

Contributors are expected to:

- discuss technical and clinical claims with precision
- distinguish evidence from inference
- critique implementations without attacking people
- protect patient privacy and confidential information
- disclose uncertainty, conflicts, and safety implications
- support newcomers with actionable review feedback

Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, doxxing, deliberate clinical misinformation, and publication of sensitive data are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `patrick@trandermatology.com`. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with appropriate confidentiality.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and participation that violate these standards.
